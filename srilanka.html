🇱🇰 Sri Lanka: India's Closest Exotic Escape

Sri Lanka sits just 80km off India's southern tip, yet feels like a world away — ancient temples, lush tea plantations, pristine beaches, and warm hospitality make it a compelling sell. Post-economic crisis recovery is complete: hotels have reopened, prices are exceptional, and the government is actively courting Indian tourists. With direct flights from multiple Indian cities and visa-on-arrival, Sri Lanka offers the best value-per-experience ratio of any destination in this portfolio.

Why Sri Lanka, Why Now
Post-crisis pricing: Sri Lanka's 2022 economic crisis forced hotels to slash rates. Recovery is underway but pricing has not normalised — this is the golden window to contract. Properties that were $200/night are now available at $70–80/night net.
Indian government support: India–Sri Lanka bilateral tourism is a government priority. Air India and IndiGo have added direct routes. India is now Sri Lanka's #1 source market.
Cultural connection: Sri Lanka's Buddhist, Hindu, and Tamil heritage resonates deeply with South Indian travellers. Sigiriya, Temple of the Tooth, and Adam's Peak are on every Indian traveller's bucket list.

Must-Have Contract Terms
  • Net rates — minimum 30% margin (post-crisis pricing allows this)
  • Indian/South Indian vegetarian meals — daily, no extra charge
  • Tamil-speaking staff at beach/south coast properties
  • Free cancellation — 7 days FIT (be firm — Sri Lankan hotels very flexible)
  • Airport transfer — Colombo airport is 30km from city (include at net)
  • Driver + guide — include at net rate for cultural circuit packages
  • Safari jeep — Yala/Udawalawe wildlife included for family packages
  • Ayurveda spa — minimum 1 complimentary treatment per stay for wellness
  • Group rates — 1 FOC per 10 (more generous than other markets)
  • Whale watching — Mirissa, included Nov–April season for beach packages

📅 18-Month Sri Lanka Contracting Roadmap

Sri Lanka needs a circuit-based approach — one itinerary covers multiple hotels (Colombo → Cultural Triangle → Kandy → South Coast). Contract the circuit, not individual properties.

🚀 Phase 1: Contracting (Now → Jul 2026)
Week 1–2 · April 2026
Aitken Spence + Jetwing chain calls
These 2 local chains cover 20 of our 32 target properties. One call each unlocks the full circuit. They have India sales teams ready.

Shangri-La Colombo, Cinnamon Grand, Taj Samudra, Hilton Colombo. Essential for MICE and group arrival/departure nights.
