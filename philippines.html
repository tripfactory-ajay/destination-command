🇵🇭 Philippines: Southeast Asia's Most Underrated Indian Market

The Philippines is one of Southeast Asia's fastest-growing destinations for Indian travellers — driven by world-class island beaches, English-speaking hospitality, and a warm, service-oriented culture that resonates deeply with Indian visitors. Palawan has been voted the world's best island multiple times. Manila is a growing MICE hub. Direct flights from Indian cities are expanding rapidly, and the government has made visa-on-arrival available for Indian passport holders.

Why Philippines, Why Now
World's best islands: Palawan and El Nido consistently rank #1 globally for beaches. Indian honeymooners and luxury FIT travellers are discovering the Philippines as an alternative to the Maldives at a fraction of the cost — private island resorts for $200–300/night vs $600+ in the Maldives.
English everywhere: The Philippines is one of the world's largest English-speaking nations. Zero language barrier for Indian travellers — menus, staff, signage all in English. Hugely underrated selling point vs Thailand or Bali.
Boracay comeback: After the 2018 cleanup closure, Boracay has reopened cleaner and better than ever. Indian travellers who avoided it are now returning, and hotel inventory is strong with competitive rates.

⏰ Shoulder season rates — Jun–Sep 2026. Philippines low season (Jun–Oct) offers 50–60% off rack. Best time to contract peak (Nov–May) allotments at discounted rates. Avoid Holy Week (April) for group travel.
Strategy: Philippines is dominated by Ayala Land Hotels (local luxury), international chains in Manila, and independent boutique resorts in Palawan and Boracay. Go direct to resorts in the islands — chain relationships matter most in Manila.

📅 18-Month Philippines Contracting Roadmap

Philippines requires a circuit approach — Manila gateway + island destination. The island resorts are mostly independent and very open to Indian operators. Move fast before competitors establish relationships.

🚀 Phase 1: Island Contracting (Now → Aug 2026)
April–May 2026
Palawan luxury resorts — El Nido & Coron
El Nido Resorts (islands A/B/C/D), Two Seasons Coron, Busuanga Bay Lodge. These are the aspirational properties that sell Philippines to Indian HNI.
May–June 2026
Boracay beach hotels
Shangri-La Boracay, Crimson Resort, Astoria Boracay, Discovery Shores. Boracay is the volume driver for groups and young Indian couples.
June 2026
Manila MICE hotels
Fairmont Makati, Raffles Makati, Sofitel Manila, Conrad Manila. Essential for MICE credibility and as gateway night hotels.
July–August 2026
Package build + agent training
Build 6 Philippines packages: Palawan Luxury, Boracay Beach, Manila+Boracay, Cebu Adventure, Honeymoon Island, MICE Manila. Train 50 agents.
