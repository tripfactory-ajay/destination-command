Why Singapore, Why Now
Structural demand + shoulder season rates = act now
MICE dominance: Singapore hosts more Indian corporate incentive trips than any other SEA destination. The Singapore Tourism Board actively subsidises Indian MICE operators — ask for STB matching grants on group bookings above 50 pax.
Shoulder season window: Apr–Sep is Singapore's shoulder season. Hotels drop rates 40–55% vs peak (Nov–Mar). This is the window to contract — then sell peak at full package price.
Indian F&B pull: Singapore has 600+ Indian restaurants across Little India and Orchard. Vegetarian and Jain travellers face zero friction — a huge selling point for Indian family and group travel that competitors struggle to match in other SEA cities.

Must-Have Contract Terms
Non-negotiables for every Singapore hotel contract
  • Net rates — minimum 25% margin FIT, 30% MICE/groups
  • No-deposit allotment — 5 rooms min for peak (Nov–Mar, June school)
  • Free cancellation — 7 days FIT, 14 days groups/MICE
  • Indian vegetarian breakfast — available daily, not just on request
  • Connecting rooms — guaranteed for family packages (3+ pax)
  • Universal Studios / MBS access — preferred partner pricing
  • Hindi/Tamil-speaking front desk or dedicated ORN contact
  • MICE inclusions — AV, 2 coffee breaks, lunch for delegate packages
  • Group rates — 1 FOC per 15 paying pax minimum
  • STB grant facilitation — hotel assists with Singapore Tourism Board MICE grant paperwork

Area Guide for Agents
What to sell and where in Singapore
🏙️ Marina Bay — Iconic Skyline
Marina Bay Sands, The Fullerton, Raffles. Business and luxury FIT. Infinity pool, casino, Sands SkyPark. Premium pricing justified.
🎢 Sentosa — Family & Resort
Universal Studios, beach clubs, cable car, S.E.A. Aquarium. Best for families. Resorts World Sentosa is the centrepiece.
🛍️ Orchard Road — Shopping Hub
ION Orchard, Ngee Ann City, Paragon. Mid-market to luxury shopping hotels. Indian shoppers' favourite area. Closest to good Indian restaurants.
🏛️ Clarke Quay / CBD — MICE
Singapore Expo, Suntec City, Raffles Place. Best for MICE and corporate groups. Walkable to convention centres and financial district.
🍛 Little India / Bugis — Budget & Culture
Indian restaurants, temple visits, budget hotels. Sell to value-conscious Indian families who want authentic food and community feel.
