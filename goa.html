🇮🇳 Goa: India's Premium Domestic Luxury Market

Goa is India's #1 domestic tourism destination and a massive opportunity for ORN to build premium hotel contracts that serve both domestic Indian FIT and international inbound travellers. The luxury resort market in Goa has exploded — properties like Taj Exotica, Leela, W Goa, and Four Seasons-standard boutique resorts are filling up with Indian HNI, destination weddings, and MICE groups. Contracting here means capturing domestic premium spend that currently bypasses ORN.

Why Goa, Why Now
Wedding & MICE goldmine: Goa hosts 3,000+ destination weddings annually — the average Indian luxury wedding spends ₹5–10Cr on venue, accommodation, and F&B. ORN is currently missing this segment. Contracting with the top wedding resorts unlocks this high-margin channel immediately.
New luxury supply: W Goa, The St. Regis Goa, Four Seasons-level independents, and boutique heritage properties have opened in the last 3 years. Indian HNI now have genuine world-class luxury options in Goa — and they're spending.
Year-round potential: While peak is Nov–Mar, Goa's shoulder (Apr, Oct) and monsoon (May–Sep) offer 40–60% discounts from luxury hotels desperate for volume. Package deals in monsoon are a unique opportunity — "secret Goa" monsoon retreats are trending.

Must-Have Contract Terms
  • Net rates — minimum 25% FIT, 30% weddings/MICE
  • Wedding packages — dedicated wedding coordinator at net rate
  • F&B minimum — negotiate reduction for ORN groups
  • Room block — guaranteed allocation peak season (Dec–Jan)
  • Free cancellation — 14 days FIT, 30 days weddings
  • Group rates — 1 FOC per 15 pax; 2 FOC per 25 for weddings
  • Airport transfer — Dabolim/Mopa to resort at net
  • Complimentary upgrade — for honeymoon bookings
  • Early check-in/late check-out — guaranteed for groups
  • Overriding commission — 2% quarterly above 150 room-nights

📅 18-Month Goa Contracting Roadmap

Goa requires a two-track approach: luxury/wedding contracts first (high margin, high value), then volume beach hotels for groups. Peak season allotment (Dec–Jan) must be secured by July 2026 — hotels fill 6 months ahead.

🚀 Phase 1: Luxury & Wedding (Now → Aug 2026)
April–May 2026
Taj Hotels Goa — all 3 properties in one call
Taj Exotica (South Goa), Taj Fort Aguada, Taj Cidade de Goa. Three most iconic Goa addresses. One Taj relationship covers all. Wedding + FIT + groups.
May 2026
W Goa + St. Regis Goa — luxury standalone
W Goa (Marriott) and The St. Regis Goa are the newest ultra-luxury properties. High demand from HNI and destination weddings. Strike before competitors.
June 2026
Leela Goa + Alila Diwa — South Goa luxury
Leela Palace is the most recognisable wedding venue in South Goa. Alila Diwa is boutique luxury. Both essential for wedding product.
July–August 2026
North Goa volume + package build
InterContinental, Marriott Goa, Hyatt Centric, Grand Hyatt. Build 6 Goa packages: Luxury FIT, Wedding Venue, Honeymoon, North Goa Groups, Monsoon Retreat, MICE. Train 60 agents.
📈 Phase 2: Scale (Sep 2026 → Sep 2027)
Sep–Oct 2026
Goa launch + peak season activation
Sell out pre-blocked Dec–Jan allotment. Christmas and New Year Goa packages are premium. Target 200 room-nights in peak window. Wedding enquiries pipeline.
Nov–Jan 2027
Peak season — weddings + HNI
Prime Goa wedding season. Target 5 destination weddings through ORN network. Average wedding = ₹8Cr spend. Even 1% referral fee is significant.
Feb–Apr 2027
Post-wedding season + IPL packages
Goa is an IPL hotspot — Mumbai Indians fans travel to Goa around matches. Cricket + beach packages for young groups. Strong social media pull.
May–Sep 2027
Monsoon luxury packages
Curate "secret monsoon Goa" packages at luxury hotels. 60% discount, lush green landscape, empty beaches. Niche but high-margin for HNI wanting exclusivity.
