Why Malaysia, Why Now
Indian diaspora advantage: 2M+ Indian-origin residents in Malaysia mean Indian food, temples, and community are everywhere. Indian travellers feel at home immediately — a huge sales point vs other destinations.
MICE opportunity: Malaysia Tourism Board offers MYR 50 per delegate subsidy for qualifying Indian MICE groups (20+ pax). KL has world-class convention centres — KLCC, PWTC — at 40% lower cost than Singapore.
Langkawi duty-free: Langkawi's duty-free status makes it a unique beach + shopping destination. Indian families love the combination. Resorts here are offering deep discounts to fill outside school holiday peaks.
