Why Japan, Why Now
Visa barrier removed: Japan visa-on-arrival for Indian passport holders has unlocked pent-up demand. Japanese hotels are scrambling to build India-ready product — vegetarian menus, Hindi signage, Indian breakfast options.
Yen at 30-year low: The JPY/INR rate makes Japan 35-40% cheaper than 5 years ago in rupee terms. A luxury ryokan stay that felt unaffordable is now competitive with UAE premium packages.
Cherry blossom boom: March-April is Japan's peak and Indian demand for sakura season is exploding on social media. Pre-block now for March 2027 — properties fill 12 months ahead.

Must-Have Contract Terms

  • Vegetarian breakfast — Japanese or Western veg options daily

Area Guide
🗼 Tokyo — Urban & Modern
Shinjuku, Shibuya, Ginza. MICE, shopping, technology. Best base for first-time India visitors. Excellent transport hub.
⛩️ Kyoto — Cultural & Heritage
Temples, geisha districts, ryokans. Best for honeymoon and cultural FIT. Arashiyama, Fushimi Inari, Gion.
🦌 Osaka/Nara — Food & Fun
Dotonbori, Universal Studios Japan, Nara deer park. Best for family groups. Food capital of Japan.
🌊 Hakone/Fuji — Nature & Onsen
Mt Fuji views, hot springs, ryokans. Best for honeymoon add-on and wellness seekers. Day trip or overnight from Tokyo.

📅 18-Month Japan Contracting Roadmap

Japan requires more lead time than SEA markets — Japanese hotel decision-making is consensus-driven. Start chain relationships now. Cherry blossom season (March 2027) needs allotments confirmed by September 2026.

🚀 Phase 1: Foundation (Now → Sep 2026)
April–May 2026
Chain outreach — Marriott Japan, IHG Japan, Accor Japan
3 chains cover 25 of our 40 target Tokyo/Osaka properties. Japan Tourism Agency also has an India desk — contact them for introductions.
May–June 2026
Tokyo Tier 1 luxury contracting
Aman Tokyo, Park Hyatt Tokyo (Lost in Translation hotel), Peninsula Tokyo, Four Seasons Marunouchi. Iconic properties that sell themselves to Indian HNI.
June–July 2026
Kyoto ryokan + boutique hotel blitz
Hoshino Resorts KAI, Aman Kyoto, Ritz-Carlton Kyoto, Four Seasons Kyoto. Pre-block cherry blossom allotment for March 2027.
August–September 2026
Package build + agent training
Build 6 Japan itineraries: Tokyo City, Tokyo-Kyoto Classic, Golden Route, Family Osaka, Honeymoon Ryokan, Mt Fuji + Tokyo. Train 60 ORN agents.
📈 Phase 2: Scale (Oct 2026 → Sep 2027)
Oct–Nov 2026
Launch Japan product + autumn foliage packages
Momiji (autumn leaf) season Oct–Nov is Japan's second peak. Launch packages to ORN network. Target 200 room-nights in first 60 days.
Dec 2026–Feb 2027
Winter Japan packages — ski + illuminations
Hakone winter onsen, Niseko ski (with IndiGo/Air India connectivity), Tokyo Disneyland winter parade. Niche but high-margin.
Mar–Apr 2027
Cherry blossom peak season
Sell out pre-blocked allotment. Premium pricing 2x normal rate. Target 300 room-nights across contracted properties in peak 3 weeks.
May–Sep 2027
Expand to Hokkaido + Hiroshima
Add Hokkaido (summer lavender fields, dairy country) and Hiroshima/Miyajima (history + heritage). Differentiate from competitor Japan products.
