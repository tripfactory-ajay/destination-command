Why Vietnam, Why Now
E-visa breakthrough: Vietnam's 3-minute online e-visa (90 days) has removed all friction. Combined with falling flight prices, India to Vietnam is now as frictionless as Thailand.
Ground floor opportunity: Indian operators have barely scratched Vietnam's potential. The market is 3–4 years behind Thailand in Indian penetration — first movers get the best rates and chain relationships.
Vegetarian-friendly: Vietnam's Buddhist food culture means vegetarian options are genuinely available at most restaurants. Da Nang and Hoi An in particular have excellent Indian-run restaurants catering to the growing market.

📅 18-Month Vietnam Contracting Roadmap

Vietnam requires a circuit-based approach with open-jaw flights (fly into Hanoi, out of HCMC). The classic 8–10 night circuit is the most popular Indian sell. Build the circuit package first, then expand to beach extensions.

🚀 Phase 1: Circuit Build (Now → Aug 2026)
April–May 2026
Marriott Vietnam + Accor Vietnam chain calls
Covers Sofitel Legend Metropole Hanoi, Park Hyatt Saigon, Pullman Da Nang, Novotel Hoi An, Renaissance Saigon — 12 key properties in 2 calls.
May 2026
Ha Long Bay cruise companies
Paradise Cruises, Indochina Sails, Seaplane + cruise combos. Ha Long Bay requires cruise operator contracts, not hotel. Target 3 cruise operators.
June 2026
Hoi An + Da Nang boutique luxury
Four Seasons The Nam Hai, Anantara Hoi An, InterContinental Da Nang. Best honeymoon properties — these are the ones clients request by name.
July–August 2026
Circuit package build + agent training
Build 6 Vietnam circuit packages: Classic 8N, Grand 12N, Honeymoon Central, Family North, MICE HCMC, Phu Quoc Beach. Train 60 agents.
📈 Phase 2: Scale (Sep 2026 → Sep 2027)
Sep–Oct 2026
Vietnam campaign launch
Target North and West India (markets where Vietnam is less known). Lead with Mỹ Sơn Hindu temple angle for South India. Target 150 room-nights month 1.
Nov 2026–Feb 2027
Peak season — best weather Central Vietnam
Da Nang and Hoi An peak weather Nov–Feb. Pre-block beach resorts. Package with Hanoi + Ha Long circuit for 10N tours.
Mar–May 2027
Phu Quoc island expansion
Add 5 Phu Quoc beach resorts (JW Marriott, InterContinental, Radisson Blu) for island extension packages. Best weather Mar–Sep.
Jun–Sep 2027
Sa Pa + Ninh Binh — north Vietnam expansion
Sa Pa trekking, Ninh Binh (Halong Bay on land). Adventure and cultural packages for repeat Indian visitors seeking something different.
