Must-Have Contract Terms
Non-negotiables for every Thailand hotel contract
  • Net rates — minimum 25% margin FIT, 30% groups
  • No-deposit allotment — 5 rooms min for peak dates
  • Free cancellation — 7 days FIT, 14 days groups
  • Indian meal plan — buffet with Indian section or à la carte
  • Early check-in / late check-out complimentary for ORN
  • Jain/vegetarian meals available on 24h notice
  • English + Hindi-speaking front desk contact
  • Group rates — 1 FOC per 15 paying pax minimum
  • Overriding commission — 3% quarterly above 100 room-nights
  • Rate parity — ORN rates not undercut on OTAs
