Why Bali, Why Now
The opportunity is structural and growing
Indian honeymoon demand for Bali has grown 3x in 3 years. Bali's mystical-romantic image is deeply embedded in Indian popular culture via Bollywood and social media.
Direct flights from Mumbai (Air India), Delhi (IndiGo), and Bengaluru (AirAsia) have removed the connectivity barrier. Bali is now as accessible as Phuket for Indian travellers.
IDR at historic lows: The Indonesian rupiah has weakened significantly vs the Indian rupee, making Bali packages 20–30% cheaper in rupee terms than 3 years ago. A premium villa honeymoon for 2 is now under ₹2L all-in.

Must-Have Contract Terms

  • In-villa dining — Indian/veg options available in villa
  • Spa credit — minimum USD 50 per couple for Tier 1 packages
  • FOC policy — 1 FOC per 10 for groups, applicable to group leader
