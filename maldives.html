Why Maldives, Why Now
India is #1 source market: Indian arrivals to Maldives grew 18% in 2024. Every major Maldives resort now has a dedicated India Sales Manager. They will negotiate hard for committed volume operators.
Seaplane slot scarcity: During Indian peak season (Oct–Apr), seaplane slots to remote atolls become scarce. Pre-block resort + seaplane transfers simultaneously — this is where early contracting gives the biggest advantage.
Indian F&B evolution: Post-COVID, virtually every major Maldives resort now has Indian curry on the menu. Several have full Indian chefs. Jain and vegetarian options have become standard. This removes the last major objection Indian clients had.

Must-Have Contract Terms
  • Net rates — minimum 30% margin (AI packages allow higher margins)
  • All-inclusive package preferred — Indian clients love all-in pricing
  • Indian cuisine — daily Indian option at main restaurant minimum
  • Jain meals — available on 48h advance notice
  • Seaplane transfer — pre-blocked slots linked to room booking
  • Speedboat alternative — for shorter transfers where seaplane not needed
  • Honeymoon setup — overwater villa, flower bath, rose petal path, amenity
  • Free cancellation — 14 days (more restrictive market, negotiate hard)
  • Complimentary snorkelling gear — gear included for all villa types
  • House reef access — direct from villa, no boat needed

Atoll Guide for Agents
✈️ North Male Atoll — Quick Access
30min speedboat from airport. Most affordable. Anantara, Centara, Velassaru. Best for shorter stays and budget-luxury.
🛥️ South Male + Ari Atoll — Mid-Range Luxury
45–60min speedboat or 15–20min seaplane. Four Seasons Kuda Huraa, COMO Maalifushi, W Maldives. Most popular Indian market choice.
🛩️ Remote Atolls — Ultra-Luxury
30–45min seaplane. Soneva Fushi, Gili Lankanfushi, Cheval Blanc. For HNI only. Total privacy, best reefs, highest prices.
🏝️ Baa Atoll — UNESCO Biosphere
UNESCO Biosphere Reserve. Manta ray season Aug–Nov. Four Seasons Landaa Giraavaru. Best for marine enthusiasts.

📅 18-Month Maldives Contracting Roadmap

Maldives is a year-round destination but Indian peak (Oct–Apr) requires allotments 6 months ahead. Seaplane slot pre-booking is as important as room allotment — both must be secured simultaneously.

🚀 Phase 1: Rapid Contracting (Now → Aug 2026)
Week 1–2 · April 2026
Top 8 Indian-market resorts — direct outreach
Anantara Dhigu, W Maldives, COMO Maalifushi, Centara Grand, Conrad Maldives, Baros, Kuramathi, Sun Siyam Iru Veli. Each has India Sales Manager.
Week 3–4 · April 2026
Ultra-luxury tier — HNI Indian market
Soneva Fushi, Gili Lankanfushi, Cheval Blanc Randheli, Velaa Private Island. For ORN's top 1% clients. Higher margins, smaller volume.
May 2026
TMA (Trans Maldivian Airways) seaplane pre-block
Contact TMA directly to pre-block seaplane slots for Oct–Apr 2026–27 peak. This is critical — seaplanes are the bottleneck, not rooms.
June–August 2026
Package build + Maldives agent training
Build 6 Maldives packages: Overwater Honeymoon, Luxury FIT 5N, HNI Escape, Family Reef, Budget Overwater, All-Inclusive Supreme. Train 40 agents.
📈 Phase 2: Scale (Sep 2026 → Sep 2027)
Sep–Oct 2026
Peak season activation — pre-booked allotment
Sell out pre-blocked Oct–Dec rooms for Indian honeymoon and Diwali season. Target 100 villa-nights in first 30 days of launch.
Nov–Dec 2026
Christmas + NYE premium packages
Overwater villa NYE packages at 2.5x standard rate. Indian families willing to pay ₹5–10L for this experience. Sell out fast.
Jan–Apr 2027
Peak season scale + renegotiation
Renegotiate top 8 resorts with volume data. Add Baa Atoll manta ray packages for Apr–Sep season. Target 200+ villa-nights in peak quarter.
May–Sep 2027
Low season value packages + new openings
Build low-season Indian value packages (20–30% cheaper). Add new resort openings to portfolio. Target 5-star properties opening 2026–27.
